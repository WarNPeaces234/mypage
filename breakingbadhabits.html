What Do We Do?

ScriptEd equips students in under-resourced schools with fundamental coding skills and professional experiences that together create access to careers in technology.




The Problem

  • The technology industry is growing faster than ever, but new, diverse talent is becoming harder and harder to find

  • Not enough high school students are trained in computer science skills

  • 80% of STEM students choose their careers in high school or earlier
